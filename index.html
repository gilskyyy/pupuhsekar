Daftar Video Pupuh

Pupuh Bali is a traditional form of Balinese poetry and music originating from Bali, Indonesia. It involves rhythmic verse that is often accompanied by traditional musical instruments. Each pupuh has a specific rhythm and meaning, usually related to stories, legends, or profound cultural values.

  • Pupuh Ginanti

    Describes the beauty and harmony of nature.

  • Pupuh Sinom

    Conveys moral messages and profound life philosophies.

  • Pupuh Ginada

    Highlights feelings of love and longing.

  • Pupuh Durma

    Tells stories of heroism and struggle.

  • Pupuh Dandang

    Describes journeys and challenges faced.

  • Pupuh Pangkur

    Illustrates stories of love and yearning.

  • Pupuh Semarandana

    A cheerful work filled with spirit and celebration.

  • Pupuh Pucung

    Delivers humorous and light-hearted stories.

  • Pupuh Maskumambang

    Contains spiritual themes and significance.

  • Pupuh Mijil

    Captures the beauty of nature and daily life.
